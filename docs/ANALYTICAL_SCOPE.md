# Analytical scope

This repository is a Level A figure-replication package. It reproduces the
published visualizations and computed manuscript tables from frozen accepted
analytical outputs. It also preserves the exact source representation of three
author-curated contextual or methodological tables.

It intentionally excludes:

- the full posterior archive and Bayesian sampling stack;
- model-development and failed-run history;
- internal plans, audits, prompts, agent files, and teaching material;
- manuscript drafting and reviewer-response files;
- claims of prospective remaining-useful-life prediction;
- claims that `C/D` identifies a unique electrochemical mechanism;
- claims of validating a new sensor architecture, EIS implementation, or
  online battery-management deployment.

The aggregate A1 analysis uses cycle-level median `C/D` values after a
seven-battery support rule. H5.1 uses per-battery regular-eligible observations.
Comparisons must preserve this distinction.

The posterior-predictive intervals describe observations and are distinct from
latent-mean trajectory intervals. Battery 11 remains a retained,
boundary-sensitive localization case rather than a failed measurement.
